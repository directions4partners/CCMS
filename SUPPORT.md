# CCMS Support

Thanks for using **CCMS (Cloud Customer Management System)**!

**Project repository:** https://github.com/directions4partners/ccms

## How to Get Help

1. **Search existing issues** to see if your question or bug was already reported.
2. **Open a new issue** with a clear title and details:
   - What you expected vs. what happened
   - Steps to reproduce (minimal repro if possible)
   - Version info, logs, and environment details

## Private or Account-Related Questions

If your question **should not** be public (e.g., contains sensitive data or business context), contact us directly at **freddy@directions4partners.com**.

## Response Expectations

- We strive to **triage new issues within 5 business days**.
- Severity-1 production outages affecting many users will be prioritized.
- Volunteers respond as availability permits; community contributions are welcome!

## Security Issues

Please **do not** open public issues for potential vulnerabilities. Follow the process in [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md).
